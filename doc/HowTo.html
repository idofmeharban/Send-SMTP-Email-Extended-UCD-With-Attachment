You may also have to enter correct values for "Mail Server Username" and "Mail Server Password". 5) Upload the releases/Send-Smtp-Email-Extended-1.0 file as a plugin into UCD under "Settings" tab -> "Automation Plugins" -> "Load Plugin". 6) Click the "Processes" tab. 7) Press the "Create New Process" button. 8) Give the process a name. 9) Press the "Save" button. 10) Under the "Design" tab of the process, locate the "Send SMTP Email Extended" step under Utilities -> Misc. 11) Drag over the "Send SMTP Email Extended" step. 12) Enter values in "Name", "To List", and "Message" then edit any other parameters as needed. Provided attachment information if needed 13) Press the "Save" button. 14) Drag an arrow from "Start" to "Send SMTP Email Extended" and from "Send SMTP Email Extended" to "Finish". 15) Press the "Save" button under Tools. 16) Press the "Close" button on the dialog box that opens. 17) Go back to the "Processes" tab. 18) Press "Run" on the row of your newly created process. 19) Select your resource from the drop down menu. 20) Press the "Submit" button.
